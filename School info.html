About the School

Learn, Grow, and Inspire

This website was created by students of Santa Laurensia School as part of a learning project to share educational content and knowledge.

Founded in 1994, Santa Laurensia School is a well-known Roman Catholic SPK school located in Tangerang, Indonesia. The school is recognized for its progressive approach to education and its commitment to developing students academically, socially, and morally.

The school offers a holistic education program from preschool to senior high school. Learning is delivered through a bilingual and trilingual curriculum that helps students develop strong communication skills and global awareness.

With campuses in Alam Sutera and Suvarna Sutera, the school emphasizes character formation, critical thinking, creativity, and other important 21st-century skills. Students are encouraged to become responsible individuals who are prepared to contribute positively to society and the global community.
